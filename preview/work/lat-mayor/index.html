L.A. mayor's race

This interactive explains the 2022 Los Angeles mayoral election at the neighborhood level, allowing readers to search their address and see results for their precinct, city council district and surrounding areas.

The experience combines geospatial joins, custom Mapbox visualizations and small-multiple charts to turn complex civic data into something personal and intuitive.

Live project
Enter an address to see precise results for your voting precinct, council district and neighborhood
Side-by-side maps compare your neighborhood with your city council district.
The tool also compares your neighborhood with demographically similar areas, highlighting where support for Bass or Caruso was strongest.

Each bar represents a neighborhood, colored by margin of victory, revealing how voting patterns shift across Latino, Black, Asian, white and income groups.
